About Me

Hi, I’m Andrea, an AI Engineer at Focoos AI specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision.

With a background in Data Science and Engineering from Politecnico di Torino and a research thesis completed at the Mixed Reality Lab at ETH Zürich, I now focus on building innovative AI solutions. My research work included hand tool pose estimation during surgical activities.

I've developed a strong foundation in deep learning, computer vision, and building efficient model architectures. Beyond my professional work, I enjoy tackling complex data challenges and publishing personal coding projects.

You can find my detailed work in the Projects section. I also maintain a dedicated University page where you can find comprehensive course summaries from my engineering studies.
